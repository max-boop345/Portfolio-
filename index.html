About Me

I am a 21-year-old Civil Engineering student at École des Ponts ParisTech (ENPC), currently on exchange at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).

My journey began with 3 years of intensive Maths and Physics in MPSI and MP* at Lycée Masséna in Nice, where I developed a strong foundation in algorithms, complexity analysis, and numerical methods.

I am particularly fascinated by the Mechanics of Continuous Media (MMC) and its application to bridges, buildings, and structural analysis. My goal is to become a Structural Analysis Engineer, leveraging numerical simulation and AI to optimize designs.
